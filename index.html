Key Features

Hybrid PQC Handshake

Combines NIST‑standardised PQC KEMs (Kyber) with classical ECC for compatibility and layered security. Migration‑friendly and resistant to harvested‑data attacks.

Protocol Extensions

Open specifications and reference implementations for Matrix and XMPP. Designed to be respectful of existing clients and to support graceful negotiation.

Mobile & Low‑Power Optimisations

Benchmarked implementations, platform‑specific optimisations, and fallbacks for constrained devices to keep latency low.

Audited & Transparent

All code is open; we budget for external audits and publish benchmark data and audit reports for community review.

Developer Friendly

Language‑agnostic libraries (Rust/C bindings) and SDKs for easy integration into existing clients and services.

Usable Security

Intuitive key management, device onboarding, and recovery flows to ensure real users keep their accounts secure without friction.
